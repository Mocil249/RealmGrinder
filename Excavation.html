There are a total of 24 quests/key artifacts that can be used to unlock races, research and research slots.



There are a total of 78 historical artifacts, which when combined with other artifacts can unlock new content at various stages of the game flow.

Note: The starting race-specific historical artifacts cannot be discovered when using a prestige race. (Example: Goblin artifacts cannot be mined when additional dwarves are used.)
